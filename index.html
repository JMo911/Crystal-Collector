CrystalsCollector!

Game Instructions
Please read before you begin:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amoun of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. If your score goes over the random number, then you lose!

The value of each crystal will not be shown. You must click on the crystal to find out what its value is.

The game resets after each round. The desired number will change, and each crystal will have a new value.
